What is Campus Art?

Art is meant to be seen. The BYU-I campus is home to many great pieces of art, but it is often difficult to find that art, and once you find it and your curiosity peaks, it's impossible to find any information about it! This is where we come in. Our team has created an easy-to-use web app that allows you to find and learn about all the art on the BYU-I campus. Using the interactive map you can find buildings that contain artwork. Once you've selected a building, the artwork and information are just one click away. With this web app, we hope to give everyone a chance to learn about the great art you can find on the BYU-I campus.
