About Us

We are a group of five students attending BYU-Idaho. Walking around campus, we noticed that the artwork on campus can be a bit confusing. There is no way to view the art online or to locate specific paintings.

We decided to change that. We made this Campus Art page to help the local students of BYU-Idaho locate their favorite art. We used Django as the framework for the website, and made it modular so we could gradually add more and more buildings to the database. We learned a lot about building websites from frameworks, and had to go through a large number of trial-and-error processes to get it working right.
